My Food Review

Cook from Home

The Product

This is a cooking app that allows users to view food recipes
and help the users to learn and cook healthier meals.

The Duration

Start : August 2022

End : November 2022

The Problem

Everyone can learn how to cook. However,
for some people, cooking is a little more challenging. There are a ton of cooking apps out there, but it's hard to find just one that is easy to use, and offers several delicious , nutritious and healthy recipes.

The Goal


To create a cooking app that is fun, easy to use, and offers several delicious, nutritious and healthy recipes

My Role


My role is UX researcher and UX designer

My Responsibility

Conducting user research.
creating wireframes, prototyping and mockups.
Conducting usability study.

User Research Summary

I conducted the interviews and created empathy maps for the users. After reviewing their needs and wants, I started designing the wireframe . The primary group of people identified during research were people who are interested in cooking and amateur cooks who still has lot to learn.

Personas

Problem statement:
Tshering is a college student who wants to improve her cooking skills while learning new dishes because she wants to save money and time.

Usability Study Findings

User wants to learn and explore new cuisines and cooking techniques

No matter what their skill level, people believe that they have room to grow

People are more likely to explore new things when they have guidance.

Time is a big factor when people consider what to cook.
