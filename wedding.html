WedCheck App

The Product

This is a complete wedding checklist and resource for
a busy couple or wedding planner!

The Duration

Start : August 2022

End : November 2022

The Problem

Brides have a limited amount of time to ﬁgure out
everything that would need to be done prior to the date of the big day,
and have resources everywhere around them, but are unsure what to do with all of it.

The Goal


Completed wedding checklist app will ensure a successful wedding day which will provide less stress to busy brides and couples

My Role

My Responsibility
